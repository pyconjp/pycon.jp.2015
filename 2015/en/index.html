Jul 19 2015

Call for proposals have been extended to 20th July 2015.

by Hironori Sekine

We are currently accepting proposals for this PyCon JP 2015, which is scheduled for 9-12 October. The deadline for submission is 20th July.

  • https://pycon.jp/2015/en/talks/cfp/

The theme for this year's PyCon JP is "Possibilities of Python". We are looking for proposals in line with the theme. We will also accept proposals not strictly related to the theme. Your proposal will be reviewed by the PyCon JP committee for acception/rejection. We plan to inform you the outcome on 24th Aug. We look forward to receiving your proposal.

Jun 07 2015

PyCon JP 2015 Registration Open

by Rico Imazu

Registration for PyCon JP 2015 is open! Buy tickets on the PyCon JP 2015 connpass page.

We are currently selling early bird tickets and the reduced price of Personal 8,000 JPY(normal: 10,000 JPY) and Business 12,000 JPY(normal: 15,000 JPY). The price includes the after-party so be sure to register early and get the reduced rate.

We are also selling patron sponsorships which include admission. If you can, please help support PyCon 2015 by becoming a patron sponsor.

For more information about the registration process and ticket types see the event registration page.

See you in PyCon JP 2015!
